ffcompress-mp4(1) -- compress mp4 video 
===========================================================

## RETURN VALUES
2 on `Not enough arguments`

## SYNTAX
Good CRF values for x265 are from 26 to 30. The scale is logarithmic. 
`ffcompress-mp4 <path/to/file1> <CRF value>`
